About us

INDIAN FOOTBALL FANS ASSOCIATION , found by a group of football enthusiasts in the year 2018 with an idea of uniting the Indian fan clubs of each football club in the football world. Started by Kaushik who is an ardent Liverpool supporter and karthik who is a manchester united fan for ages, the Association started to grow. The Association reached a total of 10 fan clubs all over the cities of India by the end of 2020 which is considered as a huge achievement as this is a group of fan club in india which is a country filled with millions of people who follow cricket. Join with us and lets celebrate the most beautiful game!

Perks

The members of our association has certain perks which you would enjoy

ASSOCIATION GATHERINGS/DISCUSSIONS

This is a new area of work for the IFFA but we are keen to support the development of our association and will seek to establish a network so that like-minded groups can share ideas and experience simultaneously helping in the development of the association.

membership id

The members upon registering will be provided with a membership ID. The membership ID will be the soul proof of your membership in our association. It can be used in several places as a proof .

fan club's matchday watchalongs

The members of the respective fan clubs will be invited for matchday watchalongs in several places. You can support your favourite club's game along with your fellow supporters. Party and enjoy!

creating your club's fan club

Interested in setting up your own supporters’ group? Then we can help with that with model rules and constitutions for supporters’ trusts and independent supporters’ associations. You're welcome, you can do that here.

Become member of the official fans club

As our association is registered in UEFA and FIFA , we can get the fan clubs an official membership .Which makes you an official member if you join the registered fan clubs.

oppurtuinity to visit the home ground

The association registers the fans club as one of the official fans club of the football club which gives you an oppurtunity to visit the homeground as an official member of the team's fan club.
